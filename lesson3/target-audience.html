Target Audience

Our target audience is business owners in our area, who will likely be middle-aged or older relatively high-income people accessing our website on work computers, and also business patrons of our community, who will likely be middle-aged middle-income people accessing our website on mobile devices.

Personas

  • A 40-year-old upper-middle-class woman looking to become a member to boost awareness of her new small business
  • A 50-year-old upper-class man who owns several restaurant franchises and is evaluating our area and its economy to decide whether he should open a new one in town

Scenarios

  • What benefits are included with a gold membership?
  • Is it going to rain this week?
  • How many people live in the area?
  • Who do I talk to if I want to cancel my membership?
  • What kind of restaurants are in town?
