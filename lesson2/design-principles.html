Design Principles Document

Allie Langdorf

Contrast

Hyer

flyhyer.com

This website uses black text and icons on a mostly white background, classic max contrast strategy. The large black menu buttons in the corner (with thin lineart for the icons) draw attention to themselves, which is good, since the customer needs to know where they are.

White Space

Woven

https://wovenmagazine.com

I'm not sure if this is an example of white space, per se, but I like how they leave so much of the picture from the main article untouched by text. It lets the picture shine (and I mostly picked it because I like the picture) and draws the viewer in.

Repetition

Laura Worthington Design

lauraworthingtondesign.com/fonts

Even though the whole purpose of this page is to show off a bunch of different fonts, the repetition of the same color scheme, layout, and font for the other text throughout the page makes it still cohesive.
