Select Membership Level

Non-Profit - Free

Only available for non-profits. Includes Bronze level personnel benefits and Silver level advertising benefits.

Bronze - $200/year

For small businesses that want more exposure. Includes:

  • Attendance to monthly networking lunches for two people
  • Attendance to yearly chamber meeting for two people
  • Directory listing on our website
  • Small advertisement in annual Chamber catalogue

Silver - $500/year

For larger businesses that want more involvment in the Chamber. Includes:

  • Attendance to monthly networking lunches for four people
  • Attendance to yearly chamber meeting for four people
  • Directory listing on our website and custom landing page
  • Half-page advertisement in annual Chamber catalogue

Gold - $1000/year

For established companies that want strong connection with the Chamber and the community. Includes:

  • Attendance to monthly networking lunches for six people
  • Attendance to yearly chamber meeting for six people
  • Directory listing on our website, custom landing page, and logo on our website
  • Full-page advertisement in annual Chamber catalogue
