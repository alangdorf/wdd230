Bountiful Foods Site Plan

Allison Langdorf

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to advertise our business and product to potential customers, and to allow customers to purchase custom fruit drinks. The site should be bright and refreshing to appeal to consumers.

Target Audience

The target audience of this site is distributors, restaurants, and indivdual consumers who are interested in buying local produce. They will likely be looking for things like organic and non-GMO produce and sustainable farming and business practices.
